Stocket is a web application designed to integrate real-time stock market data with relevant tweets from your trusted sources. By combining prices taken from Bloomberg's extensive database, natural language processing, and an intuitive user interface, Stocket will help you get a clear picture of what is happening in a particular market at any given time.

Just enter the company you'd like to monitor as well as the Twitter handles and search terms you'd like to track, and we'll take care of the rest.
